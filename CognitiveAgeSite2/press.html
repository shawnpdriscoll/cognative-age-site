The Cognitive Age Initiative is a global movement advocating for human dignity, cognitive rights, and economic fairness in the age of artificial intelligence. This page provides journalists, researchers, policymakers, and the public with official statements, background materials, and media resources.

Official position

Our stance on the Cognitive Age

Artificial intelligence is built on the collective cognition of humanity. The Initiative asserts that human cognitive contribution is economic infrastructure — and must be recognized, protected, and compensated.

Our work centers on the World AI Constitution and the Global Cognition Accord, two foundational documents designed to guide global governance and economic policy in the age of intelligence.
